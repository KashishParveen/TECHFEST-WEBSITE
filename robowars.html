Description

Experience the ultimate test of engineering and strategy in Robowars. Teams will build and battle robots in a high-stakes, action-packed competition. With a variety of obstacles and challenges, your robot's endurance, power, and control will be pushed to the limit.

Whether you're a seasoned builder or a newcomer, this event offers an exhilarating experience for all participants and spectators alike. Do you have what it takes to claim the championship title?
