Description

Welcome to the Tech Expo, the flagship event of TechFest 2024! This two-day exhibition showcases cutting-edge technologies, innovative projects, and groundbreaking research from students, faculty, and industry partners.

Visitors will have the opportunity to explore a wide range of tech demonstrations, attend insightful presentations, and engage with the brilliant minds behind the latest technological advancements. From AI and robotics to sustainable tech and beyond, the Tech Expo offers a glimpse into the future of technology.

Don't miss this chance to network with industry professionals, discover potential career opportunities, and be inspired by the incredible possibilities that technology brings to our world!
